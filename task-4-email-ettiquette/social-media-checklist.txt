# Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## ✅ DO’s

1. Maintain a respectful and professional tone while posting or commenting online.
2. Use strong privacy settings to control who can view your personal information.
3. Verify information before sharing news, links, or posts.
4. Think before posting — consider how your content reflects your personality and values.
5. Use social media for learning, networking, and building a positive digital presence.
6. Credit original creators when sharing their work or ideas.

## ❌ DON’Ts

1. Do not share personal details such as address, phone number, passwords, or OTPs.
2. Avoid posting offensive, abusive, or discriminatory content.
3. Do not spread misinformation or unverified rumors.
4. Avoid engaging in online arguments or cyberbullying.
5. Do not post inappropriate photos or content that may harm your academic or professional reputation.
6. Never click suspicious links or download files from unknown sources.
