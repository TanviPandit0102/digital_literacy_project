## Project Overview

This repository contains my Digital Literacy Portfolio developed as part of the Digital Literacy course. The project was completed under the role of a *Student Digital Ambassador*, with the objective of helping students understand responsible and effective use of digital tools.

The portfolio demonstrates practical digital literacy skills including creating professional online profiles, using collaboration platforms, practising professional communication, and spreading cybercrime awareness. Each task reflects both technical learning and personal understanding of digital responsibility.

---

## Project Tasks

### Task 1 – Digital Literacy Awareness Infographic
Created a one-page infographic explaining the concept of digital literacy and its importance for students. The infographic highlights safe internet practices, useful digital tools, and professional online behaviour using a visual design platform.

📁 Folder: `task-1-presentation/`

---

### Task 2 – Student Digital Portfolio
Established a professional digital presence by creating and updating profiles on platforms such as GitHub, LinkedIn, and Kaggle. These platforms help in building a long-term academic and professional identity.

📁 Folder: `task-2-portfolio/`
